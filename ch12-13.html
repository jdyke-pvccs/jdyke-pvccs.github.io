  • Existence - The system might not yet exsit, so it is not possible to experiment directly on the actual system.
    		•
  • Physical realization - The system is not constructed from entities that can be represented by physical objects.
    		•
  • Safety - It might be too dangerous to exxperiment on the actual system or a physical replica.
    		•
  • Speed of construction - It might take too much time to construct a physical model.
    		•

    Ethical behavior - Some physical models have serious moral and ethical consequences, perhaps the best known being the use of animals in medical research.
